Accessing Your Match

Find and open your tournament fixture in COMET

  1. Log into COMET at comet.canadasoccer.com
  2. Navigate to 'Clubs' tab
  3. Click 'My Next Matches'
  4. Find your match and click your club tab at the top
2

Loading Available Players

Access your registered player pool for the competition

  1. Click 'Load Available Players' button
  2. Review your player pool on the left side
  3. Check for any players marked in RED (suspended)
  4. Verify you have minimum players available
3

Creating Your Start List

Select starting players and assign roles

SL Starting Lineup (11 players)
GK Goalkeeper (minimum 1 required)
CP Captain (1 required)
L Lineup but not starting (substitutes)
  1. Select players and assign roles using the codes above
  2. Click the arrow (‚Üí) to move players to Start List
  3. Use checkboxes and trash icons to move players back if needed
4

Adding Team Officials to Bench

Select coaching staff and officials for the match

  1. Press 'Edit' button above Club Officials section
  2. Use dropdown menus to select officials for each position
  3. Ensure you meet minimum requirements
Officials in RED have active suspensions and cannot be selected

Post-Match Activities

Follow-up tasks after the match

  1. Match Summary Report will be emailed to club contacts
  2. Access match reports through COMET if needed
  3. Any disciplinary reports will be sent via email
  4. Follow email links to access cases in COMET
  5. Prepare for next match if applicable

Common Issues & Solutions

Player appears in RED and cannot be selected

Player has an active suspension and is ineligible for this match

Cannot find 'Load Available Players' button

Ensure you are on your club's match page and have proper permissions

Lineup submission shows error

Check that you have exactly 11 starters, 1 goalkeeper, and 1 captain designated
