2. Your CLUB in COMET

Setting up your club profile and basic information

2.1

Login to COMET

Access COMET at comet.canadasoccer.com with your provided credentials.

If you have a new account, select 'Forgot Password' to have a new password sent to your email.
2.2

Accessing Your Club Page

  1. Select 'Clubs' from the main menu
  2. Select 'My Club' in the dropdown menu
  3. Your Club page will open
  4. Edit details by choosing 'Edit'
2.3

Adding Your Club Logo

  1. Click 'Edit', then click on 'Documents' to add or edit club logo
  2. In the Documents window, choose 'Club Logo' from the menu
  3. Click 'Choose' to upload club logo from the computer
  4. Once uploaded, you will see the newly uploaded logo under Club Profile
2.4

Adding CCES Certificates

  1. In the Documents window, choose 'Other' from the menu
  2. Click 'Choose' to upload required CCES documents from computer
Required: All required CCES certificates must be uploaded to the club page.
2.5

Creating Team Kits

  1. Click 'Team Kits' on your Club page
  2. Click 'Add' to add a new team kit
  3. Use dropdown menus to select Kit Type (Home/Away/Other/Goalkeeper) and Kit Pattern
  4. Use Colour Selector to select colours for shirt/shorts/socks
  5. Press 'Save'
Requirement: Teams must have at least 2 different and distinct sets of uniforms. Goalkeepers need 2 uniforms distinguishable from other players plus 3 distinct colour socks.

3. Your competition TEAM

Managing players and team officials for competition

3.1

Player Registration Process

IMPORTANT: Before registering a player, ALWAYS CHECK if they already exist in the system using Quick Search or advanced search in the Players module.
3.2

Checking Existing Players

  1. Use Quick Search at top left of COMET window
  2. Type player name - if multiple results, you'll see a dropdown list
  3. If player exists, verify information and update if necessary
  4. Create a New Registration to attach to existing profile
3.3

Creating New Player Registration

Required fields:

  • National ID (or passport/driver's license)
  • Gender
  • Family Name
  • First Name
  • Common Name (first + last name for game sheets)
  • Passport
  • Nationality
  • Date of Birth
  • Country of Birth
  • Position
  • Picture upload
  • Club
  • Discipline
  • Level (Amateur)
  • Date from (registration date)
3.4

Registration Status Flow

ENTERED
→
SUBMITTED
→
CONFIRMED

You must edit and save as submitted. Only CONFIRMED players can be added to competition.

3.5

Team Officials Registration

Similar process to players. Required fields include:

  • National ID
  • Gender
  • Family Name
  • First Name
  • Common Name
  • Passport
  • Nationality
  • Date of Birth
  • Country of Birth
  • Picture upload
  • Club
  • Discipline
  • Registration Type
  • Age Category
  • Date from
3.6

Adding Players to Competition - CORRECTED

  1. Navigate to 'My Competitions' and select your competition
  2. Go to 'Clubs' tab, find your club, click 'Player' button
  3. Select CONFIRMED players and enter shirt numbers
  4. Click 'Add Selected', then change status to SUBMITTED

COMPETITION-SPECIFIC ROSTER LIMITS:

National Championships (Adult/Senior): 15-21 players
National Championships (Youth U-17/U-15): 15-20 players
PDP Championship: 16-20 players
Masters Championship: 15-21 players
Futsal Championship: 10-14 players
Required: All competitions require minimum 1 goalkeeper
3.7

Adding Team Officials to Competition - UPDATED

  1. Select 'Officials' button beside your Club name
  2. Select officials from your Club pool
  3. Click 'Add Selected' when finished

TEAM OFFICIALS LIMITS BY COMPETITION:

National Championships (Adult/Senior): 2-5 officials
National Championships (Youth U-17/U-15): 2-5 officials
PDP Championship: 2-6 officials
Masters Championship: 2-5 officials
Futsal Championship: 2-4 officials
Required: All competitions require at least one official of same gender as division

4. Your matchday lineup

Creating and submitting match day lineups

4.1

Accessing Match Information

  1. On Clubs tab, click 'My Next Matches'
  2. On match page, find your club tab at the top
4.2

Creating Your Start List

  1. Click 'Load Available Players'
  2. Select player roles:
    • L - lineup but not starting
    • SL - starting lineup
    • GK - goalkeeper
    • CP - Captain
  3. Click the arrow to confirm players on your match sheet
  4. Move players between Available and Start List using checkboxes
4.3

Players Under Suspension

Players under active suspension will appear in RED and cannot be selected for the lineup. They will not be able to be checked off and added to your lineup.
4.4

Adding Team Officials to Bench

  1. Press 'Edit' button above Club Officials section
  2. Use dropdown menu to select appropriate individual for each position
  3. Respect maximum officials allowed per competition type
Note: Only individuals registered to your Club will appear in dropdown. Official limits vary by competition - see Section 3.7 for details.
4.5

Final Submission

CRITICAL: To submit your game sheet, you MUST click 'Save as CONFIRMED' button.
  • Once confirmed, your lineup is submitted and cannot be changed
  • This must be done no later than 45 minutes prior to match kick-off
  • Your submission is stamped with your electronic signature
Note: COMET accounts should never be shared - electronic signatures are individual
4.6

Post-Match Activities

  1. Access Match Summary - PDF copies available through online system
  2. Disciplinary Reports - automatic email notifications for any sanctions
  3. Follow links in emails to access cases in COMET

Important Notes

National ID: National ID Number is required - use passport or driver's license if no ID number available
Common Name: Common name appears on game sheets - ensure first and last name is entered
Eligibility: Players must be eligible for current season league matches
Reinforcement: No reinforcement players after qualifying for competition
Roster Limits: Roster limits vary significantly by competition type - always check requirements
Security: COMET accounts should never be shared - electronic signatures are individual
Deadlines: All deadlines must be met - late submissions may not be accepted
